Red Rock Ridge Land Exchange Proposed Action

The Forest Service proposes a land exchange on Example National Forest. The action would convey
approximately 120 acres of National Forest System land out of federal ownership and acquire
approximately 160 acres of non-federal land for consolidation and public management.

The exchange would reserve a public trail access easement and address existing road access needs
across the affected parcels.

The project area includes a perennial stream, riparian corridor, and mapped wetlands that require
review before the land adjustment proceeds.

The Forest Service has not completed cultural resource survey for the parcels, and tribal
coordination may be needed before the project record is complete.
