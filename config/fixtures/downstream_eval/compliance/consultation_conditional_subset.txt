Environmental Assessment under the National Environmental Policy Act and NEPA. The EA describes environmental effects, alternatives, consultation, public comment, significant effects, purpose and need, affected environment, environmental consequences, coordination, environmental review, schedule, objection period, and a publicly circulated review.

The project includes a biological assessment, Endangered Species Act Section 7 consultation for threatened and endangered species with the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, National Historic Preservation Act Section 106 review, 36 CFR part 800 consultation, Montana SHPO coordination with the State Historic Preservation Officer, cultural resource and cultural resources review, adverse effect review, and historic properties documentation.
